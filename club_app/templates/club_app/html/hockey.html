Hockey Page Welcomes You

Field hockey is a team sport of the hockey family. The game can be played on grass, water, artificial turf, synthetic field, and indoor boards. It is played between two teams of eleven players each, using a hooked stick to hit a round ball into the opponent's goal. Hockey is India's national sport and has a rich history in the country. India has won the most Olympic gold medals in field hockey, making it one of the most celebrated sports in the nation.
