Welcome to PlayForge Sports Club

With a passion for promoting health, fitness, and sportsmanship, Ramagya Sports Academy, in collaboration with White Canvas India, launched Noida's First and Biggest Sports League - NSL. This spectacular event encouraged individuals of all ages, from 4 to 71 years old, to participate in 12 exciting indoor and outdoor sports. The event, spanning 27 hours, created history with over 3,062 participants and earned a National Record as the largest family sports league in India.
