Tennis Page Welcomes You

Tennis is a racket sport that can be played individually against a single opponent or between two teams of two players each. Each player uses a tennis racket strung with cord to strike a hollow rubber ball covered with felt over or around a net and into the opponent's court. Tennis is an Olympic sport and is played at all levels of society and at all ages. The sport can be played by anyone who can hold a racket, including wheelchair users.
