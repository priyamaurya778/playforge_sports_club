Athletics Page Welcomes You

Athletics is a group of sporting events that involves competitive running, jumping, throwing, and walking. The most common types of athletics competitions are track and field, road running, cross country running, and race walking. Athletics is among the oldest of all sporting activities. The first recorded instances of organized athletics competition date from Ancient Greece. The modern Olympic Games, which began in 1896, made athletics one of its foundation sports, and athletics events have been held at every Summer Olympics since.
